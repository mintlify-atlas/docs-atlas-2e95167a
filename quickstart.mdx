title: "Get started with PathForge.ai"
description: "Learn how to generate your first personalized AI career roadmap with PathForge.ai in under two minutes — no account or sign-up required."
---

PathForge.ai requires no account, no installation, and no payment. You fill out a short profile form, and the AI generates your personalized career roadmap on the spot. The whole process takes under two minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open PathForge.ai in your browser">
    Open the PathForge.ai application in your browser (or your self-hosted instance). On the landing page, click **Generate Your Career Roadmap** to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the 3-step profile form">
    The form collects your profile across three steps:

    **Step 1 — Basic info**

    Enter your name and select your education level from the dropdown:

    - High School
    - Undergraduate 1st Year through Final Year
    - Graduate / Masters
    - PhD
    - Bootcamp / Self-taught
    - Working Professional

    **Step 2 — Your spark**

    Describe your interests and list the skills you already have. Be as specific as you can — the more detail you provide, the more relevant your roadmap will be.

    **Step 3 — Ambition**

    State your career goal. For example: "Become a machine learning engineer at a product company" or "Land a frontend developer role at a startup within 12 months."

    <Tip>
      Specific career goals produce better roadmaps. Instead of "I want to work in tech," try "I want to become a data engineer specializing in real-time pipelines." The AI uses your goal to shape every recommendation it generates.
    </Tip>

    Once you complete all three steps, click **Submit** to send your profile to the AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your personalized dashboard">
    After a few seconds, you're redirected to your dashboard. Your roadmap includes:

    - **Career recommendation** — your suggested career path with salary range and job market outlook
    - **Skills to learn** — a prioritized list of skills to develop
    - **Technologies** — specific tools and frameworks to learn, with priority levels
    - **Projects** — portfolio project ideas to demonstrate your skills
    - **Courses** — curated learning resources matched to your gaps
    - **Roadmap phases** — a structured, time-boxed plan broken into milestones

    <Note>
      Your results are stored in `sessionStorage`, not a database. They persist for your current browser session but will not be available after you close the tab. To get a new roadmap at any time, return to the home page and generate a fresh one — it only takes seconds.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
