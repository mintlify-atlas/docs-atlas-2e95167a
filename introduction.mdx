PathForge.ai uses the Llama 3.3 70B model to turn your background, interests, and goals into a concrete, step-by-step career roadmap. You describe where you are and where you want to go — PathForge.ai maps the path between them, including the skills to build, technologies to learn, projects to build, and courses to take.

## Who is it for?

PathForge.ai is built for anyone navigating a career in tech or adjacent fields:

- **Students** — whether you're in your first year of university or wrapping up a bootcamp, PathForge.ai helps you translate your education into a focused job-ready strategy.
- **Career changers** — if you're moving into tech from another field, PathForge.ai identifies the fastest route from your existing experience to your target role.
- **Working professionals** — if you want to level up, pivot to a new specialty, or break into a senior role, PathForge.ai gives you a structured plan grounded in where you already are.

## Mission

PathForge.ai exists to democratize career guidance. Quality career coaching has historically been expensive or inaccessible. PathForge.ai makes it available to anyone with an internet connection — regardless of background, location, or resources.
