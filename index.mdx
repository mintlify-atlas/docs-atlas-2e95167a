PathForge.ai turns your background and ambitions into a concrete, actionable career roadmap. Powered by the Llama 3.3 70B model via Groq, it analyzes your profile and generates a personalized plan complete with skills to learn, technologies to master, projects to build, and courses to take — all in a matter of seconds, with no sign-up required.

## Generate your first roadmap

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open PathForge.ai">
    Navigate to the PathForge.ai application and click **Generate Your Career Roadmap** on the landing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your profile">
    Complete the three-step form: enter your name and education level, describe your interests and current skills, then define your career goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your roadmap">
    The AI engine processes your profile and returns a comprehensive, personalized roadmap on your dashboard — ready to follow immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PathForge.ai is completely free and requires no sign-up. Your roadmap is generated fresh each time based on your current profile.
</Note>
